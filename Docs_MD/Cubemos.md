# Cubemos

The [RealSense CHOP](<./RealSense_CHOP.md> "RealSense CHOP") uses the [Cubemos RealSense Skeleton Tracking API](<https://www.intelrealsense.com/skeleton-tracking/>) to provide skeleton tracking data. 

Cubemos can also operate standalone with TouchDesigner, taking video streams in and outputting channels containing body tracking data. 

**NOTE** : You must purchase from Cubemos a Cubemos license to use the skeleton tracking.
