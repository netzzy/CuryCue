# Mouse Click

**LMB** is the same as **left-click** or **lclick** , meaning "click the left mouse button". 

**MMB** is the same as **middle-click** or **mclick**. **NOTE** : If you don't have a middle mouse button, press Alt+Right-click. 

**RMB** is the same as **right-click** or **rclick** , and is sometimes called the "context menu" or "options menu". 

TouchDesigner works with the three-button mouse. 

See also [RMB Menu](<./RMB_Menu.md> "RMB Menu").
