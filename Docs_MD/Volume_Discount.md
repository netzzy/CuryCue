## Purchasing

To place a Volume Discount order via the online store follow these steps: 
1. Email sales@derivative.ca with the quantity and type of licenses you'd like to purchase.
  2. The Derivative team reply with a quote based on your order.
  3. Reply back to confirm or alter the purchase details.
  4. Derivative will add a one time automatic discount code to your account and email to confirm once it's available. _(Discount codes are valid for 1 month from the date they are added.)_
  5. Add the licenses to your cart and click "Checkout" to view the discount.
  6. Complete the billing details and the licenses will automatically be delivered to your account.

## 12-month Look-back Policy

Our 12-month look-back policy ensures customers receive the best possible pricing based on their purchase history over the last year. If a customer buys 11 or more TouchPlayer licenses, we count the total number of licenses purchased within the previous 12 months to determine the pricing tier. For example, if a customer purchased 20 licenses on January 10th, 2024, and then orders 15 more on October 24th, 2024, the new order would be priced as licenses 21 to 36. This allows the customer to benefit from a deeper discount based on their cumulative purchases within the past 12-month period. 

## FAQ's

**Are there any discounts on TouchDesigner licenses?**  
There are no discounts on TouchDesigner Commercial or TouchDesigner Pro licenses.  
  
**Can I combine different license types for a bundled discount?**  
No. Volume Discounts are based on a multiple license purchase of a single type.  
  
**How do I qualify for Educational licenses?**  
[Educational](<https://derivative.ca/UserGuide/TouchDesigner_Educational>) licenses are available to students and educational institutions. Educational has same features as a Commercial license, but are for educational non-paying/non-commercial use only. Students and school administrators must register for an Educational license and be approved by Derivative before being able to purchase the license. Register [Here](<https://derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-educational>).  
  
**Can I purchase by wire transfer?**  
Yes. Email sales@derivative.ca and we'd be happy to assist you with a manual order.
