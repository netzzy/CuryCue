# Connector

The notches on the left and right of each [Node](<./Node.md> "Node") are Connectors that let you [Wire](<./Wire.md> "Wire") the output of one [Operator](<./Operator.md> "Operator") to the input of another operator of the same [Operator Family](<./Operator.md> "Operator").   
  
The notches on the top and bottom of [3D Object Components](<./Object.md> "Object") and [Panel Components](<./Panel_Component.md> "Panel Component") are Connectors that tie the components of each sub-[Family](<./Operator_Family.md> "Operator Family") in a [Hierarchy](<./Hierarchy.md> "Hierarchy"). 

See also [Wire](<./Wire.md> "Wire"), [Hierarchy](<./Hierarchy.md> "Hierarchy").
