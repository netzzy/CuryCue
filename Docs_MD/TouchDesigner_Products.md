# Products

There are 4 types of license available for TouchDesigner. 

**[TouchDesigner Non-Commercial](<./TouchDesigner_Non-Commercial.md> "TouchDesigner Non-Commercial")**
* **non-commercial** license for personal and educational use at no charge.


**[TouchDesigner Educational](<./TouchDesigner_Educational.md> "TouchDesigner Educational")**
* **educational** license available to students and educational institutions. Same features as Commercial, but only for non-commercial uses. Price $300 usd.


**[TouchDesigner Commercial](<./TouchDesigner_Commercial.md> "TouchDesigner Commercial")**
* **commercial** license for paid projects, price $600 usd.


**[TouchDesigner Pro](<./TouchDesigner_Pro.md> "TouchDesigner Pro")**
* **pro** license including support, price $2200 usd.


  
For a table comparing the features of Non-Commercial, Commercial, and Pro, see [Licensing](<./Licensing.md> "Licensing"). 

## **TouchDesigner Non-Commercial**

[TouchDesigner Non-Commercial](<https://www.derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-non-commercial>) an edition of TouchDesigner available at no charge (free) for personal and non-commercial use. 
* TouchDesigner Non-Commercial is free for non-commercial use **ONLY**. (Precisely, "You are not receiving money or compensation for your work with TouchDesigner")
  * image resolution is limited to 1280x1280. (Exceptions: Images that are 1 or 2 pixels high can be any resolution, for use in ramps. See Also Commercial below).
  * does not include a subset of Pro/Commercial Operators (such as [Shared Memory OPs](<./Shared_Memory.md> "Shared Memory"), [C++ OPs](<./Category-C++.md> "Category:C++"), SDI OPs, etc).
  * keys are 1 time use and not transferrable between computers. Each account is given 10 keys, contact us if you run out.
  * does not include any direct support, [forum](<https://www.derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-educational>) support only.


For a comparison of features, see [Licensing](<./Licensing.md> "Licensing"). 

## **TouchDesigner Educational and Commercial**

TouchDesigner Educational and Commercial have identical feature sets, they differ only in their intended use-case. 

### Educational

Available to students and schools for **non-commercial and educational uses only**. (Precisely, "You are not receiving money or compensation for your work with TouchDesigner") 

Students and school administrators must register for an Educational license and be approved by Derivative before being able to purchase the license. [Register Here](<https://www.derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-educational>)
* Regular Educational license $300 USD per license. Can be used with [Key Manager Dialog](<./Key_Manager_Dialog.md> "Key Manager Dialog") or [License Dongle](<./License_Dongle.md> "License Dongle") (not included)
  * [Floating Cloud](<./Floating_Cloud_Licenses.md> "Floating Cloud Licenses") Commercial license $450 USD per license
  * Purchase TouchDesigner Educational in the [STORE](<https://www.derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-educational>).
  * [Volume Discounts](<./Volume_Discount.md> "Volume Discount") are available for TouchDesigner Educational.


See also [Educational Setup](<./Educational_Setup.md> "Educational Setup") for more information about setting up TouchDesigner at educational institutions. 

### Commercial

A Commercial (or [Pro](<./TouchDesigner_Pro.md> "TouchDesigner Pro")) license is **required for any project or work done with TouchDesigner that you receive money or compensation for**. 

Some examples include a) you are working on a paying project, b) you use TouchDesigner to promote your work to create income, c) you or a team use multiple computers to create parts of a larger commercial project, in all these cases a Commercial license is required for all computers that are used on these projects. 
* Regular Commercial license $600 USD per license. Can be used with [Key Manager Dialog](<./Key_Manager_Dialog.md> "Key Manager Dialog") or [License Dongle](<./License_Dongle.md> "License Dongle") (not included)
  * [Floating Cloud](<./Floating_Cloud_Licenses.md> "Floating Cloud Licenses") Commercial license $900 USD per license
  * Purchase TouchDesigner Commercial in the [STORE](<https://www.derivative.ca/product/touchdesigner-commercial>).

### Features Unlocked
* image resolution and compositing at any resolution the hardware supports
  * create H.264/H.265 movies in realtime (requires Nvidia GPU)
  * stream textures via RTSP, RTMP, or SRT using the [Video Stream Out TOP](<./Video_Stream_Out_TOP.md> "Video Stream Out TOP") (requires Nvidia GPU)
  * Up to 2 [Notch](<./Notch.md> "Notch") operators running at 1920x1080 (requires Notch license)
  * LIDAR scanner [Ouster TOP](<./Ouster_TOP.md> "Ouster TOP")
  * laser scanner [Leuze ROD4 CHOP](<./Leuze_ROD4_CHOP.md> "Leuze ROD4 CHOP")
  * all [Shared Memory](<./Shared_Memory.md> "Shared Memory") operators
  * [send](<./DirectX_Out_TOP.md> "DirectX Out TOP") and [receive](<./DirectX_In_TOP.md> "DirectX In TOP") texture to DirectX applications
  * use [TouchEngine](<./TouchEngine.md> "TouchEngine") in other applications
  * option to hide the TouchDesigner splash screen on startup
  * the Commercial license is good for one commercial key for one computer
  * keys can be moved from computer to computer
  * does not include any direct support, [forum](<https://forum.derivative.ca>) support only


See also [TouchDesigner Pro](<./TouchDesigner_Pro.md> "TouchDesigner Pro")

## **TouchDesigner Pro**

TouchDesigner Pro includes everything TouchDesigner has to offer. 

### Pro Support

Purchasing a Pro license gets you into our [Pro Support](<./Pro_Support.md> "Pro Support") program. Our team is available to help you with any programming, production, or consulting needs you have. Use our years of experience to make your life easier. 

Each Pro license includes 6 hours of Pro Support. Additional hours can be purchased for larger tasks and additional support. Please read [Pro Support](<./Pro_Support.md> "Pro Support") for more details on what the program includes. 

### Features Unlocked
* [Notch](<./Notch.md> "Notch") operators without any resolution limits (requires Notch license)
  * 3rd party Projection calibration via [Vioso TOP](<./Vioso_TOP.md> "Vioso TOP") or [Scalable Display TOP](<./Scalable_Display_TOP.md> "Scalable Display TOP")
  * [Stype](<./Stype.md> "Stype") optical tracking for cameras
  * for tracking data from [BlackTrax Motion Tracking Systems](<http://blacktrax.cast-soft.com/>): [BlackTrax CHOP](<./BlackTrax_CHOP.md> "BlackTrax CHOP")
  * sync multiple processes or systems with [Sync In CHOP](<./Sync_In_CHOP.md> "Sync In CHOP") and [Sync Out CHOP](<./Sync_Out_CHOP.md> "Sync Out CHOP")
  * hardware frame-lock ([NVIDIA Gsync](<http://www.nvidia.com/object/nvidia-sync-quadro-gsync.html>) / [AMD S400](<http://www.amd.com/us/products/workstation/graphics/s400/Pages/s400.aspx>) sync cards)
  * [Privacy Options](<./Privacy.md> "Privacy") for private .toe files and private components. Create a`[.toe](<./.md> ".toe")`project file or`[.tox](<./.md> ".tox")`component file that is inaccessible without the password. Additionally you can create components that only run with USB License Dongle. See [Privacy](<./Privacy.md> "Privacy") for more details.
  * for blending motion channels: [Clip Blender CHOP](<./Clip_Blender_CHOP.md> "Clip Blender CHOP"), [Clip CHOP](<./Clip_CHOP.md> "Clip CHOP"), [Clip DAT](<./Clip_DAT.md> "Clip DAT")
  * bug fixes to the specific build you are using
  * software fixes/RFEs are not guaranteed, but Pro users will receive front-of-the-line service.
  * [Pro Support](<./Pro_Support.md> "Pro Support") program offering direct access to Derivative's programmers and product specialists.

## Legacy Products

**077**

TouchPlayer077 and TouchDesigner077 have are no longer supported but the latest builds are available here. These products require licenses. 

[Download TouchDesignerFTE 077](<https://download.derivative.ca/TouchDesignerFTE.077.18900.msi>) This final build of TouchDesigner FTE 077. (FTE = Free Thinking Edition for learning) 

[Download TouchDesignerPro 077](<https://download.derivative.ca/TouchDesignerPro.077.18900.msi>) This final build of TouchDesigner Pro 077. 

[Download TouchPlayer 077](<https://download.derivative.ca/TouchPlayer.077.18900.msi>) This final build of TouchPlayer 077. 

**017**

TouchPlayer017, TouchMixer017, and TouchDesigner017 have been discontinued as of 2007, but are still available here. These products do not require licenses. 

[Download TouchDesigner017](<https://download.derivative.ca/touchdesigner_017_setup.exe>) This is the unlicensed version of the final TouchDesigner 017. 

[Download TouchMixer017](<https://download.derivative.ca/touchmixer_017_setup.exe>) This is the unlicensed version of the final TouchMixer 017. 

[Download TouchArt017](<https://download.derivative.ca/Assets/TouchArt/TouchArt017.zip>). This contains about 100 realtime artworks made with TouchDesigner 017. To get full MIDI and playback features, install this and then install TouchMixer 017. It can run in dual monitor via Dialogs -> Window Placement. You can even feed its video into the Touch In TOP of 077 and later. 

**NOTE:** The 017 Touch Tools packaged inside TouchArt require a license which is no longer available. Use the Unlicensed TouchDesigner 017 and TouchMixer 017 above to use the TouchArt artworks.
