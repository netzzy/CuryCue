# Generator

Operators that do not need any inputs connected (to create a meaningful output) are called Generators in TouchDesigner, like a [Pattern CHOP](<./Pattern_CHOP.md> "Pattern CHOP"). "Inputs" are both the [wires](<./Wire.md> "Wire") from other operators, and [OP References](<./Link.md> "Link") to other operators in OP parameters. 

Generators are the darker shaded operators in the [OP Create Dialog](<./OP_Create_Dialog.md> "OP Create Dialog"). 

See their counterparts [Filters](<./Filter.md> "Filter"). 

Operators may have inputs defined by [Parameters](<./Parameter.md> "Parameter") that are [Path](<./Network_Path.md> "Network Path") references, that are from any [Operator Family](<./Operator_Family.md> "Operator Family").
